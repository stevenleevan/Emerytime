Emery Game I've Played

Games I’ve played. Thoughts I’ve had.

Board Games

Chronicles of Avel

Try to place traps so that when the main monster comes in it gets slowed down so you can defeat it.

Dungeon!

Pick your character and try to collect a certain amount of gold listed on your card to win.

My First Carcassonne

Pick from 4 different colored figures and take turns picking tiles from a pile to complete the path.

Splendor

Try to get 15 points to win; perfect for family nights.

Ticket to Ride: First Journey

Try to make the longest train track to win. It’s a perfect family game.

Card Games

Pokemon Trading Card Game

Try to knock out your opponents Pokémon 6 times to win.

Sibley Backyard Birding Bingo

Pick cards then see if you have a number or a picture that matches.

Uno

A fun quick card game where you try to discard all of your cards without gaining extra cards.

Video Games

DRAGON BALL: Sparkling! ZERO

Fans of DBZ will love this game; you can play the story of almost every character from Goku to Goku Black. You can play with friends and CPUs.

Harry Potter: Quidditch Champions

Fans of Harry Potter and Quidditch will love this game; change position from Chaser, Keeper, Seeker and Beater. The game ends when you get 100 points or if your time limit runs out.

Hogwarts Legacy

Go into the Hogwarts world before Harry Potter and before Lord Voldemort. Learn and upgrade spells.

Mario Kart 8 Deluxe

A fun family racing game; if you like racing games then this is the game for you.

Minecraft

Build and craft. You can play creative or survival mode.

Rocket League

If you like racing games and soccer then you’ll love this; control a car and try to hit a giant soccer ball into the goal.
